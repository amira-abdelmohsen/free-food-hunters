About Free Food Finder

We're on a mission to reduce food waste on campuses by connecting event organizers with students in real time.

🍽️ Our Vision

Millions of pounds of food are wasted every year on college campuses. Meanwhile, students often skip meals due to cost or access. Free Food Finder bridges this gap by helping ensure no good food goes to waste.

🤝 Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped dozens of student organizations share leftover food with hundreds of students. It's more than food — it's about building community and sustainability.

📬 Want to get involved?

Whether you're an organizer, a student, or just passionate about reducing waste, we're excited to have you join us.
